# Execution Roadmap

## Now: 0-90 Days

Goal: Shrink installed waste.

Actions:

- Establish baseline for archival unit economics.
- Publish storage scorecard for executive review.
- Identify top-decile redundant marts, exports, and support extracts.
- Freeze net-new unmanaged historical copies.
- Define business-value classes and retrieval classes.

Outputs:

- Initial scorecard
- Redundant asset inventory
- Governance freeze policy
- Lifecycle classification model

## Next: 3-9 Months

Goal: Prevent recurrence.

Actions:

- Roll out creation-time classification for new retained assets.
- Move critical retrieval journeys to canonical-first service paths.
- Demote raw replay to governed exception path.
- Rebalance storage tiers based on business value and latency class.
- Create policy checks in data product launch process.

Outputs:

- Canonical retrieval service adoption plan
- Retention policy controls
- Class A/B/C lifecycle matrix
- Exception governance workflow

## Later: 9-18 Months

Goal: Operate archival economics as a standing discipline.

Actions:

- Add quarterly executive scorecard cadence.
- Embed archival economics in product launch reviews.
- Embed lifecycle review in partner onboarding.
- Automate duplicate-copy ratio monitoring.
- Treat new unmanaged copies as policy exceptions.

Outputs:

- Standing Pillar 06 operating model
- Automated scorecard
- Executive governance cadence
- Continuous footprint reduction backlog

## Decision Rights

Suggested ownership:

- Product: business-value class and retrieval product requirements
- Architecture: canonical retrieval patterns and evidence plane design
- Security and Compliance: control validation and legal hold policy
- Data Engineering: lifecycle enforcement and redundancy retirement
- Finance / FinOps: unit economics and cost reporting
